Tag

Playing tag is fun.

It is however enunciated in various way across North America. Some say it how you would most likely imagine it to sound as "tag" - like you would say "back."

In other areas "tag" is spoken with a "ae" like "bake."

Other than that, tag can be spoken in the North or South either faster in the former like the a in "ant", in the latter slower like "tahg"
